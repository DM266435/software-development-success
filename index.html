About

I set out to figure out why certain software development projects fail while others succeed. To which I found only one answer, it relies entirely on the human element rather than anything technology based. Things such as communication, budget, and workload can make or break a project. Not to mention that in our time with the rise of AI, it can be easy to forget how important human ingenuity and creativity are.
